The First Undergrad Neurotech Competition


Date: November 19th, 2016
Location: École de technologie supérieure
(1100 Notre-Dame O, Montreal, QC, CANADA, H3C 1K3)

The NeuroTechX Student Club Competition (SCC) is the first undergraduate competition aimed toward neurotechnology and biosignal student clubs. While NeuroTechX is working to help start student clubs all around the world, this first edition of the competition will bring together the teams from Montreal and Toronto in Canada for a 'coopetition' where they will compete in a friendly fashion.

At the intersection of neuroscience, biomedical engineering, electrical engineering and computer science, the competition provides students with the opportunity to gain outstanding experience in the interdisciplinary field of neurotechnology, while also getting a shot at shaping its future.

Industries like healthcare, education and entertainment are being disrupted by wearable technology and biosensors. Measuring our heart rate while exercising or sleeping, or our brain activity while meditating or focusing, are becoming common practices. This competition helps to prepare tomorrow’s skills, today.

Choose Your Challenge


The NeuroTechX SCC has two major independent components. First, the DIY challenge where students are called to explore the fundamentals and electronics behind biosignal acquisition. Second, the Consumer challenge where they are called to integrate all the multidisciplinary aspects of neurotechnologies in innovative ways.


DIY Challenge

Build your own electrophysiological signal board from the ground up using basic electronic components and focusing on EEG, EMG, EOG or ECG.


Consumer Challenge

Make an innovative and advanced project based on consumer grade electrophysiological devices, such as OpenBCI or Interaxon Muse.

Why Should You Participate?







NeuroTechnology

Neurotechnology has never been this accessible before. We want to catalyze the interest and involvement of skilled students by providing them with a precise goal to work towards and the resources to get there.

STEM Excellence

The NeuroTechX Student Club Competition promotes interdisciplinary collaboration, preparing the participants for careers and excellence in science, technology, engineering and mathematics (STEM) through self-learning, knowledge sharing, and practical experimentation.

NeuroEngineers

We believe neurotechnology is key to better understanding and to improving who we are. We aim to inspire a whole new generation of neuroengineers, who will be equipped to drive the field forward.

About NeuroTechX

NeuroTechX brings hackers, enthusiasts, researchers and experts together to drive innovation and foster collaboration at local and international scales. Our core mission is to build a strong global neurotech community by providing key resources, learning opportunities, and by being leaders in local and worldwide technological initiatives.

We believe neurotechnology is key to better understanding and to improving who we are. Join us to take part in the conversation and help shape the future of neurotechnology!

NeuroTechX has chapters in many cities around the world: Montreal, Toronto, Vancouver, Boston, Los Angeles, New York City, San Francisco, Amsterdam, Paris, Barcelona, London, Lima and Tel-Aviv.
